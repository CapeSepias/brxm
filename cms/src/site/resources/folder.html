jcr

JCR Interface Direct access to your content through the JCR API. The standard JCR interface exposed by Hippo CMS gives you direct access to your content inside the repository, and all the content management functionalities provided by Hippo CMS. This allows you to build almost any imaginable application on top of Hippo CMS. This section describes how to get started, and provides some examples on how you could use the possibilities of the JCR interface. examples index jcr
